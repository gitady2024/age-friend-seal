Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 1, 2026

The Age Friend Seal Certification Institute (hereinafter, "we", "our", or "the Institute") is firmly committed to protecting and respecting the privacy of our users, clients, and corporate partners. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, process, store, and protect personal and corporate information in accordance with the highest international standards, including the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR - EU Regulation 2016/679), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), and current regulatory frameworks for the protection of personal data in Latin American countries (such as Brazil's LGPD, Colombia's Law 1581, Argentina's Law 25.326, among others).

1. Data Controller

The data controller for the data collected through this platform is the Age Friend Seal International Certification Institute, with operational offices in Santiago, Chile, and with direct contact channels through the official email: privacidad@agefriendseal.com.

2. Data We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our self-diagnostic, advisory, and certification services in the Silver Economy. This includes:

  • Corporate Data: Name of the company or business, industry or services sector, and general operating scale.
  • Contact Person Data: First and last name, corporate email address, job title within the organization, and phone number.
  • Self-Diagnostic Data: Responses provided in the friendliness questionnaire (Physical Accessibility, Customer Service, Inclusion & Communication), resulting scores, and recommended action plans.
  • Technical Navigation Data: IP address, browser type, operational cookies essential for the questionnaire flow, and server activity logs.

3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data under the following international legal bases:

  • Consent of the Data Subject: By voluntarily entering your corporate and contact details to unlock the self-diagnostic questionnaire (from the third question onwards) and expressly accepting this policy.
  • Execution of a Contract or Pre-contractual Measures: When you request detailed information about advanced certifications (Medium or Premium Level) or simulate and contract the issuance of the Initial Commitment Seal.
  • Legitimate Interest: For the optimization of our platform, anonymized statistical analysis of corporate friendliness levels globally, and fraud prevention.

4. Purposes of Data Processing

Your personal and corporate data will be processed for the following exclusive purposes:

  1. To manage your professional registration and guarantee secure access to the self-diagnostic questionnaire.
  2. To generate and send by email the OTP (One-Time Password) verification code to validate your digital identity through the secure API of EmailJS.
  3. To calculate your global and sector scores, and draft the specific report of priority recommendations (Quick Wins) backed by international technical standards (ISO 25550, ISO 25551, ISO 25556, WHO, and ILO).
  4. To coordinate commercial contact calls and virtual/physical audits in case you request services for the Medium and Premium Levels of certification.
  5. To send communications about regulatory updates, sector longevity studies, and events of the certified companies network, provided we have your consent.

5. Data Recipients and International Transfers

The Institute does not sell, rent, or trade your personal or corporate data under any circumstances. Your data is only shared with essential third-party providers acting as data processors under strict confidentiality and security agreements:

  • EmailJS Inc. (USA): Provider in charge of sending the account verification email and OTP code. Delivery is made using TLS encryption, and email and name data are processed temporarily for message delivery.
  • Cloud Hosting and Server Providers: Companies hosting the site's infrastructure with leading physical and digital security certifications (such as AWS or Google Cloud), ensuring that their databases are encrypted in transit (SSL/HTTPS) and at rest.

6. Data Retention

We will retain your corporate and contact data as long as the commercial relationship lasts and your account remains active on our platform. The digital badge of the Initial Commitment Seal is valid for 1 year; the associated data will be kept for that period. In case of inactivity or at the user's request, we will proceed to securely delete your data, except for those that must be preserved due to applicable legal, tax, or contractual obligations.

7. Your Rights (GDPR & ARCO Rights)

As the data subject, you have the following internationally guaranteed rights, which you can exercise at any time by writing to privacidad@agefriendseal.com:

  • Access: The right to know what personal data we process about you and your company.
  • Rectification: The right to update or correct any inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Erasure / Deletion (Right to be Forgotten): The right to request the complete deletion of your account and records from our database.
  • Objection: The right to object to us processing your data for advertising or promotional purposes.
  • Portability: The right to receive your structured data in a commonly used digital format and transfer it to another data controller.
  • Restriction of Processing: The right to request temporary restriction of the processing of your data in specific dispute scenarios.

We will respond to any request within a maximum of 15 business days (or the periods provided by the local regulations of your country), at no cost to you.

8. Data Security

We implement rigorous technical, organizational, and physical security measures to protect your information against loss, theft, unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. This includes the use of secure HTTPS transmission protocols, hash mechanisms for passwords/codes, and internal access policies restricted only to the Institute's authorized auditing staff.

9. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update this Privacy Policy to adapt it to future legislative, jurisprudential, or market practice modifications. Any substantial change will be notified visibly on our platform or via direct email before the update takes effect.
